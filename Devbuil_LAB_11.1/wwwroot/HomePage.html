About Devin

Devin is the world's foremost CSS color naming guru. His blog, "CSS Through the Prism," is regarded worldwide as the single authoritative text in the world of gamut labels.
Since a fatal accident in 2004 which opened his third eye to the world of colors' true universal names, his skills have been sought by many global figures, including the Dalai Lama, Antarctic Research teams, and David Lynch.

Alongside this noble venture, he occasionally works in the world of web design and development, with a staggering one day's experience, placing him ahead of the pack.
This website will serve as the one true hub to learn more about the man, myth, and legend.

He hails from some hovel in the Thumb of Michigan. Its irridescence and bountiful nature set the stone for his hue moniker assignment skills.
